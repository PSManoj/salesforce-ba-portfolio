## 🗂️ Case Studies

### Case Study 1 — GET Pragnya Mobile App
**Role:** Project Coordinator & Lead Business Analyst
**Challenge:** Nonprofit serving neurodiverse individuals needed a mobile app with no technical infrastructure, no CRM, and no formal requirements process.

**What I did:**
- Conducted stakeholder interviews with CEO and COO to elicit, prioritize, and document MVP requirements
- Authored a full Software Requirements Specification (SRS) covering 6 modules with approval gates
- Evaluated FlutterFlow vs Jotform and Flutter vs Firebase against HIPAA compliance and launch timeline
- Managed a mid-project pivot; re-scoped deliverables without disrupting timeline
- Produced static UI mockups in Canva as visual requirements artifacts for executive sign-off

**Stack:** FlutterFlow · Firebase · Canva · Google Docs

---

### Case Study 2 — Operations CRM Design (Add On Support Services)
**Role:** CRM Business Analyst
**Challenge:** No CRM, no structured data tracking, everything manual and audit-vulnerable.

**What I did:**
- Designed participant registration and consent capture system with field logic and waiver flows
- Built QR-based check-in/check-out workflow with staff accountability and parent notification protocols
- Created RACI-style staffing matrices for 3 session types across 6 operational phases
- Developed multi-variable cost model separating fixed vs variable costs with per-participant baselines
- Analyzed 20+ venues against travel-time policy and produced viability report

**Stack:** Google Sheets · PDF documentation · Canva

---

### Case Study 3 — Salesforce Automation at Scale (EPIQ Global)
**Role:** Associate Business Analyst
**Challenge:** 700+ user Salesforce org with high manual workload, case escalation problems, data quality issues.

**What I did:**
- Partnered with PMs and stakeholders to define requirements and translate into Salesforce automation
- Deployed 25+ Flows and Process Builder automations — reduced manual tasks 28%, cut escalations 50%
- Configured roles, profiles, permission sets, sharing rules for 99.9% audit compliance
- Integrated Salesforce with external systems via REST API — reduced sync failures 35%
- Led UAT for 10+ releases with zero critical post-deploy bugs

**Stack:** Salesforce · REST API · Data Loader · Process Builder
