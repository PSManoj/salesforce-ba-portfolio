Background
Who I Am

I found Salesforce at my very first job in 2019 and it immediately clicked. I loved being part of the sprints, sharing progress with the team, and learning something new every week. The way CRM systems keep everything organized, traceable, and connected felt like exactly what I wanted to spend my career doing.

Over four years I have worked across three very different environments. At EPIQ Global, a billion-dollar legal services company, I worked inside a large enterprise Salesforce org with 700+ users building automations and integrating external systems. At Hopkins Software I worked directly with sales and marketing teams translating their goals into Salesforce Marketing Cloud workflows. At Add On Support Services I joined an organization that had no systems, no documentation, and no processes at all. I built everything from scratch.

That last experience taught me the most. When there is no existing system to follow, you have to really understand what the business needs before you can build anything. That is the part of this work I find most interesting.
