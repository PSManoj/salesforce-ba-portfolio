Background
Who I Am

I'm a Salesforce-focused Business Analyst with five years of experience across enterprise software, nonprofit operations, and early-stage startups. I started as a Salesforce administrator, earned an M.S. in Business Analytics, and discovered that what I do best sits at the intersection: understanding what a business actually needs, then making sure the system delivers it.

At EPIQ Global, I worked inside a large enterprise org serving 700+ users — building automations, leading UAT, integrating external systems. At Hopkins Software, I translated campaign strategy into Marketing Cloud workflows directly alongside sales and marketing stakeholders. At Add On Support Services, I designed an entire operational system from scratch — no CRM, no documentation, nothing — and built it all, from SRS authorship to legal policy addenda.

That experience taught me something important: the tool is secondary. Understanding the problem is everything. Whether that means writing an SRS, facilitating a stakeholder interview, or configuring a Salesforce Flow — the through-line is always the same: clarity first, then execution.
